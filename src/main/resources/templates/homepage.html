Revolutionizing Campus Security

SmartGate redefines security paradigms, offering an unparalleled fusion of Arduino-powered hardware and cutting-edge software. Designed exclusively for Bago City College, this innovative solution combines advanced access control with real-time communication.

Our system doesn't just secure; it connects and informs. With instant SMS notifications to parents and state-of-the-art facial recognition for exits, SmartGate creates a perfect synergy of security, transparency, and technological innovation in education.
